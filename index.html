(THE CRYPTOCURRENCY)

FAQ

Some would also call this the "Whitepaper"

Q: What is this?

A: a cryptocurrency based on my car

Q: Why did you make it?

A: because I can

Q: How did you make it?

A: Like many others, $PROTEGE is an ERC-20 smart contract ecosystem built on the Ethereum blockchain using Solidity and OpenZeppelin. It is open source, and as a matter of fact you can dig into all the source code here.

Q: How can I get some $PROTEGE?

A: Go to the factory, swap, or ask me nicely for some

Q: How many Protegé can I have?

A: As many as you want, so long as you are willing to wait for them to be minted, or pay $ETH for them on the swap

Q: What are the NFT's?

A: The NFT collection is a set of unique photos and artworks featuring Mazda Protegés. More info on them can be found here.

Q: How does the supply work?

A: The initial total supply was 1,000,000 Protegé, and the factory manufactures (mints) 1 Protegé per hour and parks it in the lot. Whoever drives it off the lot first keeps it! You can claim up to 1 Protegé per day. This method of distribution means supply increases linearly, but over time (rate of issuance)/(total supply) decreases. This helps tp prevent centralization by early adopters.

Q: What is the initial distribution?

A: The inital allocation of tokens is a follows: 75% of supply has been liquidity-locked at a fixed floor price in the swap. 20% of tokens are reserved for interactive airdrops, and 5% is held by me, the founder.

Q: What is the $HRSPR token?

A: each $PROTEGE token subdivides into 105 $HRSPR tokens. This exchange can by submitting an order on the swap without paying eth gas. The choice behind this denomination is a that Mazda Protegé SE 1.6 produces 105 horsepower from the factory. The $HSRPR token is here simply to provide a smaller denomination for more precise value transactions. Think of it as the "BRK:B" to $PROTEGE

Q: What are the tokenomics? (i.e. what sets it apart from the vast supply of shitcoins?)

A: As with all good cars, a Protegé burns gasoline, and the same goes for the cryptocurrency. Each time a transaction is made, the initiator of the transaction must pay a fee for fuel (which is burned from supply). Luckily though, the Protegé's 1.6L engine is extremely fuel efficient and this fee is low (5%). This fee boosts tokenomics by increasing scarcity, slightly decreasing liquidity (and in turn velocity), decreasing inflation, and decreasing volatility. These all improve the security of the asset.

Q: How does one execute such a perfect handbrake drift?

A: pure skills ;)
